Valerie's Fruit Dip

Valerie's Fruit Dip Description

Bruh its fruit dip. you dip your fruit and these descriptions on these websites make me weep for humanity.

Ingrediants

  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk

  1. Mix together frozen whipped topping, cream cheese, vanilla pudding mix, confectioners' sugar, and milk in a medium bowl. Serve chilled.
